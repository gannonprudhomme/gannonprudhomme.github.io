Texas A&M Rocket League

I am a community organizer/administrator for the Texas A&M Rocket League club, where we have more than 200+ current students and alumnus of A&M, and over 350+ members. In the club, we manage and hold tryouts for the main Texas A&M Rocket League Esports team, host online and LAN tournaments, organize teams for the various online leagues that the Rocket League creators host, and much more.
